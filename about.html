Team Avinya is the heartbeat of the Open Throttle Racing Club at ADYPU. We're a student-led team driven by a passion for electric mobility, sustainable engineering, and real-world innovation. Under the mentorship and support of O.T.R, we collaborate across disciplines â€” mechanical, electrical, and computer science â€” to design, build, and race high-performance electric ATVs.

Our team proudly represents the university at national platforms like SAEINDIA eBAJA and other ATV competitions, showcasing a blend of creativity, technical skill, and teamwork. We believe in learning by doing and pushing the limits of what's possible.

Our Journey

  • 2023: Team Avinya was founded by a group of passionate students determined to make a mark in the world of electric mobility. Despite being a new team, our hard work and interdisciplinary collaboration allowed us to compete on par with long-standing competitors.
  • 2024: Built and tested our first prototype, overcoming technical and logistical challenges. This milestone proved our ability to turn ideas into reality and set the stage for future competitions.
  • 2025: First eBAJA competition: cleared all major tests, including the Weight Test, Mechanical and Electrical Technical Inspections, and the Validation Test. Our team endured the Rainstorm Test and emerged stronger, earning recognition from industry experts and our university's Honorable Chairman.
  • 2025: Felicitated by Honorable Chairman and recognized by industry leaders for our innovation, teamwork, and resilience. Our journey is just beginning, and we are excited for what lies ahead.

Departments

Powertrain

Designing and optimizing the electric powertrain system. Our Powertrain team ensures maximum efficiency and performance, turning electrical energy into speed and reliability on the track.

Vehicle Dynamics

Suspension and steering systems for optimal performance. This team fine-tunes every aspect of handling, ensuring our ATV is agile, stable, and ready for any challenge.

Electrical

Battery management and electrical systems. The Electrical team powers our innovation, from safe battery integration to advanced telemetry and controls.

Braking

Advanced braking systems for safety and performance. Our Braking team ensures we can stop as confidently as we go, with robust, reliable systems.

Chassis & Manufacturing

Structural design and fabrication. This team brings our ideas to life, building a strong, lightweight chassis that forms the backbone of our ATV.

Static & Marketing

Documentation, outreach, and team management. The Static & Marketing team tells our story, manages our brand, and ensures our work is recognized and supported.
