About BAJA SAEINDIA

BAJA SAEINDIA is India's premier intercollegiate engineering design competition, where student teams from across the country design, build, and race single-seater all-terrain vehicles (ATVs). The competition emphasizes real-world engineering challenges, innovation, teamwork, and safety. Teams are judged on design, cost, presentation, and rigorous dynamic events including endurance, maneuverability, and acceleration. BAJA SAEINDIA is recognized by leading automotive companies and provides a launchpad for aspiring engineers to showcase their skills and creativity on a national stage.
