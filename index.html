About BAJA SAEINDIA

BAJA SAEINDIA BAJA SAEINDIA is a highly anticipated and adrenaline-fueled competition where students push their limits to engineer and construct a rugged single-seater off-road vehicle capable of conquering challenging terrains. This intense event encompasses a wide array of dynamic and static challenges, with the ultimate victor determined by a comprehensive evaluation of their performance across these diverse events.
